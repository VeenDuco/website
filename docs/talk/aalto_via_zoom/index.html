Duco Veen

I currently hold an Assistant Professor position at the department of Global Health situated at the Julius Center for Health Sciences and Primary Care of the University Medical Center Utrecht. In that capacity I’m involved in the COVID-RED, AI for Health, and Trials@Home projects. Besides that I’m appointed as Extraordinary Professor at the Optentia Research Focus Area of North-West University, South Africa. I work as Data Scientist for one day a week at Julius Clinical, a Contract Research Organization. I’m involved in the Stan project. Stan is “freedom-respecting, open-source software for facilitating statistical inference at the frontiers of applied statistics”. I work on the development of ShinyStan and have recently been elected as member of the Stan Governing Body.
